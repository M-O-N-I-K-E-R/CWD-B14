VALORANT AGENTS

In Valorant, "agents" are the playable characters or characters you can select to play as during a match. Each agent has a unique set of abilities, including a signature ability, two basic abilities, and an ultimate ability. Agents are categorized into different roles: Duelists, Initiators, Controllers, and Sentinels, each with its own playstyle and abilities.

Here's a brief overview of the roles:

Duelists: Duelists are aggressive agents focused on fragging and getting eliminations. They typically have abilities that help them engage enemies or secure kills.

Initiators: Initiators are agents who can disrupt and control the battlefield. They excel at opening up new angles of attack, blinding or stunning enemies, and providing information to their team.

Controllers: Controllers are agents that specialize in area control. They can create barriers, smokescreens, and other effects to control the map and limit enemy movement.

Sentinels: Sentinels are defensive agents who can lock down areas and provide utility for their team. They often have abilities that protect teammates, gather information, or slow down opponents.

Valorant continually introduces new agents, and existing agents may receive balance changes over time to ensure gameplay diversity and fairness. The game's evolving meta and agent roster make for dynamic gameplay and strategy.

"Astra"

Astra is a Controller agent known for her cosmic abilities. She can manipulate stars to create powerful gravity wells, smokescreens, and other spatial control effects.

"Breach"

Breach is an Initiator agent who excels at disrupting and incapacitating enemies with his seismic abilities, including stunning and blinding opponents through walls.

"Brimstone"

Brimstone is a Controller agent who specializes in area control with his arsenal of incendiary grenades, smokescreens, and orbital strike ultimate ability.

"Chamber"

Well dressed and well armed, French weapons designer Chamber expels aggressors with deadly precision. He leverages his custom arsenal to hold the line and pick off enemies from afar, with a contingency built for every plan.

"Cypher"

A Cypher is a Sentinel agent and a master of surveillance. He uses cameras, tripwires, and spy cages to gather information and lock down areas.

"Deadlock"

Deadlock is a female Sentinel agent from Norway that thrives in the cold wilderness thanks to her traps and hunting prowess. The most recent teaser showed her tackling a Radiante-infused bear but Deadlock uses the same techniques against enemy agents thanks to her intelligent technology and intense determination.

"Fade"

Turkish bounty hunter, Fade, unleashes the power of raw nightmares to seize enemy secrets. Attuned with terror itself, she hunts targets and reveals their deepest fears—before crushing them in the dark.

"Gekko"

Gekko the Angeleno leads a tight-knit crew of calamitous creatures. His buddies bound forward, scattering enemies out of the way, with Gekko chasing them down to regroup and go again.

"Harbor"

Hailing from India’s coast, Harbor storms the field wielding ancient technology with dominion over water. He unleashes frothing rapids and crushing waves to shield his allies, or pummel those that oppose him.

"Jett"

Jett is a Duelist agent known for her agility and precision. She can double jump and has throwing knives that she can use to eliminate enemies.

"Kay/O"

KAY/O is an Initiator agent designed to suppress enemy abilities. His kit includes a suppression grenade and a flashbang, making it difficult for opponents to use their abilities.

"Killjoy"

Killjoy is a Sentinel agent who specializes in deploying gadgets for area control. She can set up turrets and lockdown devices to deter and damage enemies.

"Neon"

Filipino Agent Neon surges forward at shocking speeds, discharging bursts of bioelectric radiance as fast as her body generates it. She races ahead to catch enemies off guard, then strikes them down quicker than lightning.

"Omen"

A phantom of a memory, Omen hunts in the shadows. He renders enemies blind, teleports across the field, then lets paranoia take hold as his foe scrambles to learn where he might strike next.

"Phoenix"

Phoenix is a Duelist agent who uses fire-based abilities. He can create walls of fire, flash enemies, and even resurrect himself if he's eliminated.

"Raze"

Raze explodes out of Brazil with her big personality and big guns. With her blunt-force-trauma playstyle, she excels at flushing entrenched enemies and clearing tight spaces with a generous dose of "boom."

"Reyna"

Reyna is a Duelist agent who thrives on eliminating enemies. She can become invulnerable and consume the souls of defeated foes to heal and boost her abilities.

"Sage"

Sage is a Sentinel agent known for her healing and defensive abilities. She can create barriers, slow fields, and resurrect fallen teammates.

"Skye"

Hailing from Australia, Skye and her band of beasts trail-blaze the way through hostile territory. With her creations hampering the enemy, and her power to heal others, the team is strongest and safest by Skye’s side.

"Sova"

Born from the eternal winter of Russia's tundra, Sova tracks, finds, and eliminates enemies with ruthless efficiency and precision. His custom bow and incredible scouting abilities ensure that even if you run, you cannot hide.

"Viper"

Viper is a Controller agent who utilizes poisonous gases to control areas and chokepoints. She can create toxic walls and deploy a deadly pit with her ultimate.

"Yoru"

Yoru is a Duelist agent who specializes in deception and misdirection. He can teleport, create footsteps to confuse enemies, and blind opponents with his flashes.
